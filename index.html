I'm a stay-at-home Mom with 3 little boys. On top of that, I am also a web dev student. As you can imagine, my days can get pretty hectic. This is what an IDEAL summer day looks like for me. Of course, things rarely go as planned!

Start

  • 7 AM: Wake up and change diapers.
  • 7:30 AM: Have breakfast.
  • 8 - 8:30 AM: Yusuf's arabic class.
  • 9 AM: Leave to drop Yusuf off at camp.
  • 9:20 AM: Yusuf has been dropped off, head to Gymboree with Harun and Abdullah.
  • 10 - 10:45 AM: Gymboree, fun fun fun!
  • 11:30 AM: Arrive home from Gymboree, start prepping lunch.

Middle

  • 12:30 PM: Go pick up Yusuf from camp.
  • 1:15 PM: Lunch.
  • 2 - 4 PM: Naptime/quiet time for the kids (my favorite part of the day).
  • 2 -4 PM: While kids are napping, depending on the day - clean and/or do some HW.
  • 4 - 6:30 PM: Kids up from nap, entertain kids, continue cleaning and/or HW.

End

  • 6:30 PM Start prepping dinner.
  • 7 PM: Dinner is served!
  • 7:45 PM Start getting kids ready for bed.
  • 8:15 PM: Kids in bed.
  • 8:30 - 10 PM Relax, most likely fall asleep on the couch in the middle of HW.
